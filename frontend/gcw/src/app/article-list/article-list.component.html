Collected works of Mahatma Gandhi

The collection includes Gandhi's writings, including letters, essays and personal notes and interviews.

The Collected Works of Mahatma Gandhi took roughly 40 years to be compiled.It covers a period from 1884 to 1948 in a chronological order, as they cover almost 60 years of Gandhiji's intensively active public life. Objective of the series is to reproduce Gandhiji's actual words as far as possible, as per the official site of the series by the Publications Division. This work is available as 98 pdf documents.

All the volumes have been parsed, tagged and are presented here in easy to search format. There is no change to the original articles. They are as they are published in the pdf.
